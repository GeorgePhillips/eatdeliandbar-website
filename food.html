food & wine

eat Deli & Bar offers breakfast, brunch, lunch and a variety of light snacks and sweet treats plus check out our deli shelves for a selection of gourmet products.

If time is a consideration our chilled cabinet is choc full of gourmet food delights, many of them gluten free.  If time permits enjoy a leisurely lunch or dinner from our regularly changing blackboard menu.

Fantastic coffees, healthy fruit juices & great NZ wines. We also serve a  range of perfectly chilled Monteiths and other local beers and ciders by the bottle.

Describing art is not our forte so we won't try. If you notice something on the wall you like feel free to make a purchase!

Bev Bell:  Fairlie photographer whose wonderful selection of Rural Landscapes we have pleasure in displaying.
